A recording studio for the PIC32

This project uses a PIC32 chip to play 4 different instrument sounds
with functionality for recording voice and short clips of instruments,
looping, playing and recording over loops.

Demo

By Radhika Chinni, Raymond Xu, Brandon Quinlan

Here's the three of us demoing the project, taken by Bruce Land


High level overview

Like most projects in Microcontrollers, we begin by breaking the overall task into smaller ones. The high level tasks are as follows:

  1. Display: We wanted a menu for the user to toggle between the playable intstruments, manage their recording by toggling playback or deleting the recording,
  2. Buttons
  3. Playing Sound
  4. Recording user via microphone
  5. Recording instruments to play back through looping

Also like previously, we will place the smaller tasks in threads so the system can be responsive and multitask successfully.
